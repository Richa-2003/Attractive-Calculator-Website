▪️ How to use Normal Calculator?

Steps to use Normal calculator :

  1. Go to Normal Calculator.
  2. Click the digits on the screen.
  3. Choose the operation to be performed like "+,-,*,/".
  4. Now Click another digits required on screen.
  5. Finally click "=" to get result.
  6. click "C" for cancelling or erasing the calculation.

Steps to use BMI calculator :

  1. Go to the BMI Calcultor.
  2. Enter your height in "Height(cm)" input box in centimeters.
  3. Then,Enter your weight in "weight(kg)" input box in Kilograms.
  4. Now, Click on "Calculate BMI" button.
  5. The result will be there on screen available below that button.
